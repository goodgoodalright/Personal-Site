Cafe Leblanc

A cozy coffee-lover's dream nestled in the heart of Yongen-Jaya. This kissaten affords commuter, student, and traveler alike a retreat to rest and recharge. Our famous menu, simple and refined, is beloved across the city. Coffee

Our meticulous pour-over process, from our trusted suppliers to the shop's trademark ceramic cups, evolves every day in pursuit of the perfect cup of coffee. Every bean is aged in airtight glass jars, weighed by hand to the gram, and poured through a perfect ratio of filtered spring water to coax otherwise secret flavors that will envelop your senses.
Yes, this is coffee, but it is so much more.

Curry

Famous for its rejuvenating effects, this hearty and healthy staple of the cafe is deceptively easy to make but nearly impossible to copy. Nine spices dance in harmony with a sweet and tangy mix of caramelized fruits and herbs swimming in a bath of homegrown comfort. Popular demand for our original beef plate gave rise to equally delicious vegetarian and vegan dishes.
The result, an ochre tide crashing against a bed of white rice, is nothing short of sublime.

Room for Rent

Upstairs, we offer a small, affordable living space on a seasonal basis. Easy access to the Hanamasa supermarket, onsen, batting cages, doctor's office, and subway station. Meals are discounted and rent can be offset by working part-time for the cafe.

Availability subject to change; no pets.
