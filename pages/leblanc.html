Coffee

Our meticulous pour-over process, from our trusted suppliers to the shop's trademark ceramic cups, evolves every day in pursuit of the perfect cup of coffee. Every bean is aged in airtight glass jars, weighed by hand to the gram, and poured through a perfect ratio of filtered spring water to coax otherwise secret flavors that will envelop your senses. Coffee for the soul.

Curry

Famous for its rejuvenating effects, this hearty and healthy staple of the cafe is deceptively easy to make but nearly impossible to copy. Nine spices dance with a sweet and tangy mix of caramelized fruits and herbs swimming in homegrown comfort. Popular demand for our original beef plate brought us to equally delicious vegetarian and vegan dishes. It'll steal your heart.
