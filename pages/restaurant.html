About Us

Our Tasty Story

Food - food! - is a team endeavor here at J&F Cuisine Restaurant Cafe where we aim to promote the foodstuff industry through the branches of fine ingredients and high skill. Here, we establish our menu and through new dishes integrate between different cultures. Our rich lives of travel and adventure have shown us the myriad wonders of the culinary arts and it is our mission to share morsels of that joy with you.

Oh, and if you see a really weird guy - y'know, kinda tall, big poofy-curly orange hair, usually wears a cool green cape thing, big fan of pranks - tell him we say hello!
