Still the Best: 2006

One way to start an impassioned discussion is to ask which of the The Elder Scrolls games reigns supreme. Now-obsolete game engines are but a minor factor in these arguments: dedicated teams like The Elder Scrolls Renewal Project have long busied themselves porting old assets into the iconic - if buggy - engines of their favorite game's successor.
So until Skywind and Skyblivion are completed, explore the lands of Tamriel. You know you want to.
